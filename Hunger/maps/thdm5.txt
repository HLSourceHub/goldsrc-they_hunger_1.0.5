* Installation *

You must have a full installation of Half-Life (Ver. 1.1.0.1),
and They Hunger23, to play this level. TheyHunger23.exe is
available for download here:
 ftp://ftp.dailyradar.com/levels/TheyHunger23.exe
Extract thdm5.bsp to your Half-Life directory. Start Half-Life
and choose custom game\They Hunger\ etc... to start a multiplayer
server with this map.
